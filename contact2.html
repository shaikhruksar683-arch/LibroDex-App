Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our support line directly.

Do you offer support for technical issues?

Yes, we have a dedicated technical support team available Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM EST. You can reach them at support@librodex.com or by calling our support line.

Can I schedule a demo of LibroDex?

Absolutely! We'd be happy to schedule a personalized demo at your convenience. Please contact our sales team at sales@librodex.com or fill out the contact form above.

What information should I include in my message?

To help us assist you better, please include your name, institution (if applicable), and a detailed description of your question or request. The more information you provide, the better we can help!
