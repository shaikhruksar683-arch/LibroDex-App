Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for books?

You can search for books using the search bar at the top of any page. Enter titles, authors, keywords, or ISBN numbers. Use the filters to narrow down your results by genre, publication year, or availability.

Can I access LibroDex on my mobile device?

Yes! LibroDex is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. There's no need to download any app - simply visit our website through your mobile browser.

How do I track my borrowed books?

Once logged in, visit your personal dashboard. There you'll see a section called "Currently Borrowed" which lists all books you have checked out, along with their due dates. You can also view your complete borrowing history.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely. We take data security seriously. All personal information is encrypted, and we comply with global data protection regulations. We never share your data with third parties without your consent.
